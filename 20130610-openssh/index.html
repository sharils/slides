OpenSSH 6.2

ssh-keygen

ssh-keygen [-N new_passphrase] [-C comment] [-f output_keyfile]

output_keyfile requires the existence of all folders in the path.

mkdir --parents ~/.ssh/github.com/example/
ssh-keygen -N '' -C root@example.com -f ~/.ssh/github.com/example/id_rsa
cat ~/.ssh/github.com/example/id_rsa.pub # this is your public key
